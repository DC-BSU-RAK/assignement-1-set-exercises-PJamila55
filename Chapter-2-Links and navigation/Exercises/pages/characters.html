Characters


  • Main Characters

    Carroll Shelby: A former race car driver and Le Mans winner turned car designer. He's charismatic, determined, and a visionary, tasked with leading Ford's racing program.

    Ken Miles: A fiercely talented but often difficult British race car driver. He's brilliant behind the wheel but struggles with authority and corporate politics.


  • Ford Team

    Lee Iacocca: An ambitious and forward-thinking Ford executive who champions the Le Mans project. He recognizes the marketing potential of racing.

    Henry Ford II: The powerful and often insecure head of the Ford Motor Company. He's driven to beat Ferrari and prove Ford's dominance.

    Leo Beebe: A Ford executive in charge of the racing program. He's focused on control, corporate image, and often clashes with Shelby and Miles.


  • Miles' Family

    Mollie Miles: Ken Miles's supportive and strong-willed wife. She believes in his talent and understands his passion for racing.

    Peter Miles: Ken and Mollie's son, who admires his father's racing talent.


  • Ferrari Team

    Enzo Ferrari: The legendary and enigmatic founder of Ferrari. He's a formidable figure in the racing world, representing Italian dominance and prestige.

    Lorenzo Bandini: A skilled Ferrari driver who competes against Ford at Le Mans.


  • Other Characters

    Phil Remington: Shelby's loyal and skilled chief mechanic. He's a crucial part of the team's success.
