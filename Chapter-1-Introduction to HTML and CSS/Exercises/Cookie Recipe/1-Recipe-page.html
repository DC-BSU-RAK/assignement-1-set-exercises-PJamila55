SOFT Chocolate Chip Cookies

Recipe

Prep Time: 10 minutes
Cook Time: 10 minutes
Original Recipe: PinchOfYum

Ingredients

  • 8 tablespoons of salted butter
  • 100 grams white sugar
  • 50 grams packed light brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1 egg
  • 188 grams all purpose flour (6.75 ounces)
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 168 grams chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F and microwave the butter.



  2. Mix butter and sugars until creamy, then add vanilla and egg.



  3. Mix in flour, baking soda, and salt. Form dough and add chocolate chips.



  4. Roll dough into balls and bake for 9-11 minutes until puffy.



  5. Let cookies cool for 30 minutes. Enjoy!
